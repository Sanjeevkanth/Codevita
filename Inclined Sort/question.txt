Sorting refers to ordering data in an increasing or decreasing fashion according to some linear relationship among the data items. In this question, we are going to perform sorting operation along the diagonal of a  2D matrix (x+y=n-1) where x and y are rows and column values in ascending order starting from left to right.

Sample Input
3
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
Sample Output
1 4 7
2 5 8
3 6 9
